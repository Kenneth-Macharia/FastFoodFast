About FastFoodFast

Standing at the entrance to the restaurant you are immediately inundated by sweet exotic aromas. These delicious smells are enough to make your mouth water and your stomach begin to growl. Located at 201 West Orange Street in downtown Lancaster, FastFoodFast is renowned for its unique atmosphere and even more so, for its outstanding cuisine. The building in which FastFoodFast is located, has been a dining establishment since the late 1800's.

In 1997, European Master Chef, Dale Miller bought the restaurant. At the time Miller was quoted as saying that it was his goal to make FastFoodFast one of the finest restaurants in Lancaster. According to the patrons who have been eating at the restaurant for years, he has accomplished his goal. With an accomplished culinary career, managing over 30 cook operations, he knew his dream of running his own place would be tough but he was clearly up to the challenge.
